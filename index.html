OUR PORTFOLIO

Industries We Serve

Automotive

We deliver high-performance forged, cast, and precision-machined components for diverse automotive applications. Our range includes specialized vehicle accessories, reverse camera systems, LCD monitors, tyre inflators, and USB charging solutions, along with temperature-control systems such as chillers and warmers supporting OEM assembly and testing processes.

Agricultural

For the agricultural sector, we deliver durable forged, cast, and machined components designed to withstand demanding field conditions. Our products support agricultural machinery, attachments, and equipment manufacturers who rely on robust parts to maintain high productivity and long-term performance in challenging environments.

Energy

We supply critical forged, cast, and precision-machined components for power generation, transmission, and industrial energy applications. Engineered for strength, durability, and resistance to extreme conditions, our solutions support conventional power systems, renewable technologies, and infrastructure providers demanding reliable, long-lasting performance.
