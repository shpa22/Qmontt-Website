Automotive

We supply high-performance components and engineered solutions to support a wide range of automotive applications. Our portfolio includes forged and cast parts, precision-machined components, and specialized vehicle accessories.

Components & Parts

  • Forged and cast parts
  • Precision-machined components
  • Suspension/axle/brake components
  • Housings and brackets
  • Bushings and structural parts

Temperature-Control Systems

  • Chillers and warmers for bodybuilders
  • OEM assembly and testing equipment
  • Portable refrigeration units
  • Climate control accessories

Vehicle Accessories

  • Reverse camera systems
  • LCD monitors and displays
  • Tyre inflators
  • USB charging solutions
  • MDVR systems
  • Infotainment modules

Applications

Our automotive solutions serve passenger vehicles, commercial trucks, buses, construction vehicles, and specialized fleet applications.

For the agricultural sector, we deliver durable forged, cast, and machined components designed to withstand demanding field conditions. Our products support agricultural machinery, attachments, and equipment manufacturers who rely on robust parts to maintain high productivity and long-term performance in challenging environments.

Component Types

  • Forged components for heavy-duty applications
  • Cast parts for machinery frames
  • Precision-machined attachments
  • Wear-resistant components
  • Structural elements

Key Features

  • High durability for field conditions
  • Resistance to wear and corrosion
  • Long-term performance reliability
  • Robust construction
  • Consistent quality

Equipment Supported

  • Tractors and harvesters
  • Seeding and ploughing equipment
  • Agricultural attachments
  • Off-highway vehicles
  • Farm machinery components
