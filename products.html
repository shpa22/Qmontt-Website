Complete Casting Solutions

Investment, Sand, CI/SGI & Steel castings - fully machined

Qmontt AB supplies end-to-end casting solutions to European OEMs and industrial buyers through a vetted network of certified manufacturers in India and Latin America. Our partners cover Investment Casting, Sand Casting, and Cast Iron (CI/SGI) and Steel components, with in-house machining for assembly-ready deliveries.

🔧 Materials & Processes

  • Investment Casting – Near net shapes, complex geometries, thin walls, excellent surface finish; wide alloy range including stainless, aluminium, bronze & high-performance grades
  • Sand Casting – Steel, SG (ductile) iron, grey iron; shell-mould, no-bake and centrifugal options for small to very large parts
  • Cast Iron (CI) & Ductile Iron (SGI) – Green sand, no-bake, cold-box and shell-core processes for consistent quality
  • Steel Castings – Precision steel components beyond iron grades, with machining and QA support

📐 Process Range & Capacities

  • Investment casting: 10 g → 120 kg per piece
  • Sand casting: 50 g → 3000 kg per piece
  • CI/SGI casting: approx. 400 g → 400 kg
  • Automated moulding & CNC machining lines

🛠 Machining & Finishing

"One-stop" solution for assembly-ready castings

  • Raw, semi-machined, or fully machined deliveries
  • Integrated blasting, CNC/VMC/HMC
  • Gear cutting, broaching and grinding
  • Powder painting available
  • Dimensional reports included

  • Automotive – Suspension/axle/brake components, housings, brackets, bushings
  • Industrial Machinery & Pumps/Valves – Bodies, bonnets, housings, mechanical sub-assemblies
  • Waterworks – Durable parts for water & sewage networks
  • Electrical Infrastructure – Accessories for medium- and high-voltage lines
  • Agriculture & Off-Highway – OEM & aftermarket components

Forging Solutions

Engineered for maximum strength and reliability

Forged products are engineered for maximum strength, toughness, and reliability, making them ideal for high-performance industrial, mechanical, and structural applications. Through controlled forging processes, metal is shaped under intense pressure, refining its internal grain structure and significantly enhancing its mechanical properties.

The result is a component with superior fatigue resistance, exceptional impact strength, and consistent dimensional integrity, even under extreme operating conditions.

Key Benefits

  • Superior fatigue resistance
  • Exceptional impact strength
  • Consistent dimensional integrity
  • Enhanced mechanical properties
  • Reliable performance under extreme conditions

Applications

  • Automotive components
  • Heavy machinery parts
  • Structural elements
  • High-stress mechanical parts
  • Industrial equipment
